Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to the Madoka Magica circle!Test

Please read the following rules and guidelines before participating in our community.

Content Guidelines

First and formost, this is a community dedicated to the Madoka Magica series. As a general rule of thumb, please ensure that all content you post is in some way related to the greater Madoka Magica series.

There is a General Discussion chat available for general conversations.

Respect and Harassment

This is a safe space for all. Any form of hate speech, bullying or discrimination will result in immediate moderation actions.

Targeted attacks on users, creators or staff will not be tolerated.

Inappropriate and NSFW Content

Inappropriate or NSFW content will not be tolerated in any form. This includes posts, chats, bios, usernames, requests and images. Users posting such content will be warned or potentially banned depending on the context.

Spam and Chat Activity

Blank posts, spam messages or chat flooding will be disabled. Chatrooms that are inactive for 14 days+ or created without a purpose will be disabled.

Regarding AI

AI-generated content such as posts or images are not welcome here. AI-generated content simply ruins the creative image of the community. Please ensure that content that you post contains no AI-generated material.
